Expert Tips for Long-Lasting Bouquets

Curate Resilient Blooms: Handpick flowers renowned for their longevity and resilience. Offer varieties like lilies, sunflowers, and hydrangeas known for their durability. Highlight these selections on your website to reassure customers of their bouquet's lasting beauty.

Personalized Care Instructions: Provide customized care instructions for each bouquet on your website. Include details such as ideal water temperature, frequency of water changes, and any specific handling tips. This tailored approach demonstrates your commitment to ensuring customers get the most out of their purchase.

Signature Bouquet Formulas: Showcase signature bouquet formulas that balance aesthetics with longevity. Share insider tips on flower combinations and greenery choices that not only look stunning but also hold up well over time. Consider offering pre-made arrangements based on these formulas for convenience.

Guaranteed Freshness: Assure customers of your commitment to freshness with a satisfaction guarantee. Clearly communicate your policies regarding wilted blooms or dissatisfaction with longevity. A strong guarantee builds trust and encourages repeat business.

Customer Care Resources:Create a dedicated section on your website for customer care resources. Include FAQs on bouquet care, troubleshooting guides for common issues like drooping stems, and a contact form for personalized assistance. Empowering customers with knowledge fosters loyalty and confidence in your brand.
